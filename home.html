Welcome to the Official Site of the Lunar Rabbit Discovery!

Welcome to the official site of the Lunar Exploration and Research Division (LERD), NASA's leading team dedicated to investigating extraterrestrial ecosystems.

In 2025, LERD made a groundbreaking discovery: satellite and soil data suggest the presence of a new species—Lepus Lunaris—living beneath the Moon’s surface. This website presents the mission, the evidence, and how you can get involved.

We invite you to explore the truth behind the myth of the Moon Rabbit, and to join us in unraveling one of the most extraordinary findings in modern space science.
