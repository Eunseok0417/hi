About LERD and the Lunar Rabbit Discovery

The Lunar Exploration and Research Division (LERD) is a specialized branch of NASA dedicated to deep-space biological research and extraterrestrial ecosystem studies.Unlike other departments, LERD focuses specifically on analyzing unexplained anomalies on the Moon’s surface, using data collected from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter and Artemis missions.Their recent groundbreaking report presents compelling evidence that a new species Lepus Lunaris may be inhabiting underground tunnels beneath the Moon’s surface.

Through the discovery of Lepus Lunaris, LERD challenges our current understanding of extraterrestrial life and opens new possibilities for future lunar exploration.This discovery also highlights the intersection between mythology and science, as legends about the Moon Rabbit have existed across cultures for centuries now with surprising scientific connections.
